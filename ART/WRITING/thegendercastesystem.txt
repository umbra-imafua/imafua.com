The word gender is used to describe a multitude of differing aspects of reality.
Gender is in one way used synonimously as sex, or which ever meaning of sex is intended by the speaker. Sex itself has gone through changes with the discovery of the mechanisms that create sex characteristics and the rare but existant bimodal outliers that ocour. But to simplify, in this usage a gender describes a feature of a person's naked form or a feature of their genetic makeup.
Gender is also used to denote a social role. Like shaman, teacher or servant, a gender describes what acts a person is expected to perform. This will differ from society to society.
Both this physiological basis and social expectation ties into the main way gender is used in today’s western society: A cult like a caste system that ties certain social roles to certain physical forms.
This caste system of gender survives through peer pressure and the threat of ostracization. Someone in one caste who behaves outside what is expected of their grouping may face ridicule or be isolated from the community.

A social genre is a way of grouping people with similar desires or ways of interacting.
Cliques such as ‘nerd’, ‘jock’, ‘hippie’, ‘suit’ are all social genres.
Sexual auras such as ‘top’, ‘subbmissive’, ‘kinkster’, ‘romantic’ are all social genre, these ones refering to preference for flirtasois and sexual interaction.
The difference between a social genre and a social caste is that social genre is drawn out of chosen behavior, whereas social castes are used to impose behaviors upon people.
A social genre describes the way someone chooses to act: a nerd is a nerd because they have an interest in and enjoy doing ‘nerdy’ things. A kinkster is a kinkster because they have an interest in and enjoy doing ‘kinky’ things.
A social caste is drawn out of something innate within someone. Their bloodline, their appearance, or in gender’s case; their sex. These castes are then used to assign expected behaviors to the people within them. The gender caste groups are expected to dress, act and live in certain ways.
