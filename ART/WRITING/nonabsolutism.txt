Nothing is truly binary or digital. Everything exists on a spectrum. Every rule is a rule of thumb.

The world we live in


You can never be one hundred percent sure about anything, this is sometimes called bayesian thinking. Any truth that you hold in your head should be seen as a percentage of asuradness rather than a binary definite assertion. Obviously rounding is a necessary part of information processing, especially when it comes to language and communication. Saying “I am sure that global warming is real” is not wrong
