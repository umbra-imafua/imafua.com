My Name is Imafua Cereal Umbra.

I spoke the word imafua when I was a young child. I would tell people that I had come from a place called imafua, that I had watched from within imafua and then one day chosen to inhabit a body.

I chose the middle name cereal in a seemingly futile attempt to remind myself not to take myself too seriously.

The word umbra describes the fully shaded inner region of a shadow, especially the area on the earth or moon experiencing the total phase of an eclipse. At night time you are in the umbra of the earth itself. I love the nighttime. It is the time when I always feel safest.
